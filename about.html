Who am I?

Hi, I'm Will Hardwick. I'm interested in a wide range of communication arts, media, and technology and how these fields all intersect to create innovative and exciting products, engaging software and web applications, and cohesive, refreshing brand identities for businesses and professionals alike. I myself have skillsets and work experience relevant to these fields, such as photography for both e-commerce and promotional use, social media management, creative direction, and graphic design. Presently, I am primarily focused on building upon my modest amount of skill and experience in web design and software development in hopes to gain employment in a related role.

Other than that, recently I've been into:

Photography

I'm always taking photos wherever I go. I used to enjoy doing staged, conceptual shoots, but recently I have been enjoying taking more candid and spontaneous snapshots of the people in my life and my travels. I also have a decent photo-book and magazine collection.

Anything outdoors / exploring the city

A lot of my interests and hobbies lead me to spend significant time looking at screens or flipping through pages. So, I prefer to spend the rest of my time out in the world in some capacity. This may be hiking through a wilderness area, going on a picnic, riding my bike, etc.

Listening to music

I often have music playing in the background, whether it be at home or in the car. However, I also love to spend time digging through the discographies of artists I like, artists related to them, and playlists of different genres. Right now, the artists/bands I have in heavy rotation are Labi Siffre, Nick Hakim, Jamiroquai, Sault, King Krule, and Ryuichi Sakamoto.
